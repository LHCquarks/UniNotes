We define
$$
\begin{align}
\cosh (x) &= \frac{e^x - e^{-x}}{2} \\
\sinh (x) &= \frac{e^x - e^{-x}}{2i} \\
\end{align}
$$
We get that
$$
\begin{align}
\cosh(x + y) &= \cosh(x)\cosh(y) + \sinh(x)\sinh(y)\\
\sinh(x + y) &= \cosh(x)\sinh(y) + \sinh(x)\cosh(y)\\
\end{align}
$$
and that:
$$
\begin{align}
\cosh(ix) &= \cos(x) \\
\sinh(ix) &= \sin(x)
\end{align}
$$
