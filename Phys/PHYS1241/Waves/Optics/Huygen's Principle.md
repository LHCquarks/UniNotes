Huygen's Principle states that as waves evolve in time you can treat all points of a wave as their own point source and then the new wave will be a line tangent to all these wave sources. ![[Pasted image 20250806123909.png]]
The tangent wave is called the wavefront.
## Uses
Huygen's principle is often used to solve problems involving light
